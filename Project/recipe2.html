Delicious Cupcake Recipe

Your ultimate guide to baking cupcakes that are moist!

Ingredients

  • Plain flour (aka all purpose flour)
  • Eggs at room temperature, not fridge cold
  • Baking powder
  • Milk, full fat
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Instructions

  1. Use a handheld beater instead of a stand mixer.
  2. Whip the eggs and sugar.
  3. Whisk together the flour, backing powder and salt.
  4. Gradually add the flour mixture into the egg mixture in 3 lots, mixing for just 5 seconds on speed 1 in between.
  5. Melt Butter and heat the milk
  6. Mix some butter into the hot tempering milk.
  7. Slowly pour milk mixture back into whipped eggs over 15 seconds while beating on the lowest speed.
  8. Lastly, fill cupcake liners with batter.
