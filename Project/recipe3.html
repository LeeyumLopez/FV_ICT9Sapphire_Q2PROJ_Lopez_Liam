Delicious Cookies Recipe

Your ultimate guide to make the best chewy chocolate chip cookies!

Ingredients

  • Melted butter.
  • More brown sugar than white sugar.
  • Cornstarch
  • Egg yolk

Instructions

  1. Whisk the flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt together in a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk the melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together until no brown sugar lumps remain.
  3. Whisk in the egg and egg yolk. Finally, whisk in the vanilla extract. The mixture will be thin.
  4. Pour into dry ingredients and mix together with a large spoon or rubber spatula. The dough will be very soft, thick, and appear greasy.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Cover the dough tightly and chill in the refrigerator for at least 2–3 hours or up to 3 days.
  7. Take the dough out of the refrigerator and allow it to slightly soften at room temperature for 10 minutes.
  8. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C). Line large baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats. Set aside.
  9. Using a cookie scoop or Tablespoon measuring spoon, measure 3 scant Tablespoons (about 2 ounces, or 60g) of dough for XL cookies or 2 heaping Tablespoons (about 1.75 ounces, or 50g) of dough for medium/large cookies.
  10. Roll into a ball, making sure the shape is taller rather than wide—almost like a cylinder.
  11. Bake the cookies for 12–13 minutes or until the edges are very lightly browned.
  12. Cool on the baking sheet for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, press a few extra chocolate chips into the tops of the warm cookies.
  13. After 10 minutes of cooling on the baking sheets, transfer cookies to a wire rack to cool completely.
  14. Cookies stay fresh covered at room temperature for up to 1 week.
